About

troveTaste is an experiment about digital archives overview. It uses the Trove API, the API of the big archive of the National Library of Australia.

It contents almost 250Milion items, grouped in 9 categories:

  • pictures: 4903410
  • maps: 389341
  • books: 17428770
  • musics: 2553743
  • peoples: 913785
  • newspapers: 78533033
  • articles: 142458865
  • lists: 19429
  • collections: 53215
  • Trove Total Records: 247732527

troveTaste is developed by Jaume Nualart as part of the PhD in COmmunication in the University of Canberra (DES2012-JAN2013).
